The Americas include an array of beautiful charter destinations. Sail the out-islands of the Bahamas and discover a sailor's paradise in the uncrowded Abacos or explore the 120-mile long archipelago of the Exumas, where you can visit the world-famous swimming pigs. Or, head to exotic Belize, which offers awe-inspiring natural wonders and waters that beckon for adventure.
